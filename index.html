How it works

From sketch to switch-on

A simple, transparent process — you always know what's happening and when.

01

Consult & design

We learn your brand, take site measurements, and mock up your sign before anything is cut.

02

Fabricate

Letters and panels are cut, formed and wired in our workshop using quality materials and LEDs.

03

Finish & test

Every piece is sanded, painted and lit-tested, so it looks flawless the moment it switches on.

04

Install

Our team mounts and powers your sign safely on-site — handover when it's glowing perfectly.
